Reviews:

This place has great parking and plenty of space inside it’s nautically themed interior. Clean, great views of the ocean and family run.

Food. The menu seems a little limited but, having said that, if you cook something to perfection then you don’t need many other choices. The Krabby patty is a favourite. This with medium coral bits and kelp rings was unforgettable. You could taste the love in the food.

Service. The order taker and waiter exuded something close to totally apathy. Glad this guy wasn’t cooking as it would have tasted like the crap from that dump across the road.

Rating: 3

by Sandy Cheeks
